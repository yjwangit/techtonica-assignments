Scramble Eggs with Tomatos 番茄炒蛋

Scramble eggs with Tomatos (in Chinese 番茄炒蛋 “fan qie chao dan") is one of the most common home cooking dishes in China. It's tasty and easy to cook.

  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 large potatos, coarsely chopped
  • 1 small bowl of oil
  • 5 tbs sugar
  • 2 tbs salt
  • 2 tbs chopped green onion
  • some water

Make the scramble eggs

1. Heat the oil in a pan for 1 minute, then toss the eggs in and slightly scramble it till 70% cooked, then take out to a plate and set it aside. 2.
